WRKPASS

Workpass is an app that helps companies manage their Remote/in-office employees.

Dec 202 1- Feb 2022 at 4D Maroc

What I did

User Research, Information Architecture, Logo Design, Wireframes, Prototype, User Testing, and Design Language.

The Problem

Since the beginning of COVID, some governments like France & Morocco forced companies that only 50% of their employees should be present in the office while the other 50% should be working from home.

The majority of the employees liked the idea of working remotely and they preferred to work from home during the pandemic, while some others didn’t agree because of the lack of material, or a strong internet connection in their homes, and they preferred to work from the office.

The Challenge

Our goal is to make an App that can be used by us (4D) and also can be used by other companies. The App should help them to manage the employees and define which employees will work remotely and which ones are going to be present in the office.

To make it easier to schedule meetings for both employee's working locations (Remote/in-office) manage meeting rooms and Zoom calls.
