My Resume

Testy Tigers

Testy Tigers is an NFT collection built on Solana Blockchain.

My role

Full Stack Designer

Tools

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML & CSS

Scope

JAN. 2023 - MARS. 2023 for Testy Tigers

What I did

Branding, Competitive Analysis, User Research, Information Architecture, Wireframes, Prototypes & Mockups, Usability Study, User Interface, Web Integration.

The Problem

The TT Team needs a website that will represent their brand in a way the makes people really attracted to the NFT project. Also they want to make their Roadmap clear and well defined for the investors.

Definening The User

Testy Tigers target users are people who are interested on the NFT space and especially they care more about the art side of the NFTs.

Color Scheme

I drew inspiration from the majestic Tiger color scheme. To reflect the fierce and bold personality of tigers, I opted for a darker color palette that exudes a strong and confident attitude.

Logo Design

The concept behind this logo was inspired by Testy Tigers' slogan, "Claws and Tail." I explored the idea of incorporating the tiger's tail and claws to create a double T logo that not only represents Testy Tigers but also conveys the essence of a tiger

Visual Design

I tried to make many user interfaces in different styles to find which one suits best the Testy Tigers brand and culture.
