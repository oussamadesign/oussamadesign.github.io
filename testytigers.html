Colors

The choice of colors in the Testy Tigers NFT collection, specifically Orange, Yellow, and Black, was carefully considered to reflect the essence of the tigers and establish a bold brand identity.

Orange symbolizes energy, vitality, and enthusiasm, capturing the dynamic and powerful nature of the tigers. It represents their fiery spirit and determination, conveying a sense of strength and action.

Yellow signifies optimism, creativity, and joy. It represents the vibrant and playful characteristics of the tigers, emphasizing their lively and adventurous personalities. The color conveys a sense of excitement and happiness, evoking a positive emotional response from viewers and collectors.

Black, as a contrasting color, adds depth and elegance to the palette. It represents the mystery, sophistication, and authority associated with the tigers. Black also enhances the visual impact of the collection, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant orange and yellow tones.

Collectively, these colors create a powerful and memorable brand identity for the Testy Tigers collection. The combination of bold orange, lively yellow, and sleek black reflects the essence of the tigers, captivating audiences with their energy, optimism, and allure.
