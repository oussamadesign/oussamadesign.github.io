Scope
APR. 2022 - MAY. 2022 for a Crypto Investor

The Problem

I just want a T-shirt with my pfp instead of branded merch.

User Research

  • NFT collections typically comprise 10K NFTs and boast large followings.
  • Merch drops are common among many collections.
  • NFT collectors form emotional connections with their NFTs (profile pics/pfps).
  • Desire for merchandise featuring favorite NFTs is strong.
  • Collection founders face a challenge: producing massive amounts of custom merch and waiting for sales.
  • Result: Much merchandise is limited to logos.
  • Collectors seek merchandise that embodies NFT characters, not just logos.
  • Hurdle: Collectors are busy and lack time to source local printers.

We've done 5 user interviews and a survey with a lot of NFT holders and most of them want to ROCK! merch with their pfp on them. Only few people prefer Branded Merch.
