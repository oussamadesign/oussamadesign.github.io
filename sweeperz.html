The Problem

There are a lot of NFT collections most of the have a supply of 10K NFTs per collection. and each one of them has a huge fan base. Most of NFT collection have their own Merch drop, but the problem is that a lot of NFT collectors build a strong relationship with their NFTs or what we call it their pfps. Their pfps represents them and they want to get some merch with their favorite NFTs printed on it.

‚Äç One of the biggest problems that the NFT collection founders are facing is that they can't print 10k Merch (T-shirts, Hoodies, Mugs, Sneakers...) and wait for it to be sold, that's why most of NFT collections Merch are just branding merch (T-shirt with logo, or text).

The NFT collectors want some Merch with their NFT character printed on them not just a T-shirt with logos. But they don't have time to print the Merch themeselves by looking for a local printer...

Definening The User

We've done a lot of user interviews with a lot of NFT holders and most of them want to ROCK! merch with their pfp on them. Only few people prefer Branded Merch.
